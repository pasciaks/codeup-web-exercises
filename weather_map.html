Weather Buddy

About

This is a simple weather app that uses the Open Weather Map API to display the current weather conditions for a given city. The app also displays a 5-day forecast for the city.

The app uses the Mapbox API to display a map of the city. The map is interactive and can be zoomed in and out and panned.

The app uses Bootstrap for styling and layout.

The app uses JavaScript to make AJAX calls to the Open Weather Map API and the Mapbox API.

The app uses the following JavaScript libraries:

  • Mapbox
  • Open Weather MAP API

Search with the text form by typing in a City, State, Country.

Click the home button to navigate and get forecast for your current GPS location.

Double-click on map to get a draggable, weather search icon.

High Pressure

A barometric reading over 30.20 inHg is generally considered high, and high pressure is associated with clear skies and calm weather.

Low Pressure

A barometric reading below 29.80 inHg is generally considered low, and low pressure is associated with warm air and rainstorms.

Normal Pressure

A barometric reading in the range of 29.80 and 30.20 inHg can be considered normal, and normal pressure is associated with steady weather.

Pressure systems are caused by the movement of air around the globe; they are the wind, and the wind is caused by differences in temperature in the atmosphere. Differences in temperature are caused by the sun; the sun is the engine that drives our weather.
